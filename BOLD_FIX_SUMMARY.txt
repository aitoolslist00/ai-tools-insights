┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ THE PROBLEM                                                              │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  BEFORE (❌ Broken):
  ──────────────────
  Screen showed: "Dive deep into **Mid Journey**, the leading AI..."
  
  Expected:      "Dive deep into Mid Journey, the leading AI..."
                                   ^^^^^^^^^^^
                                   (bold, not asterisks!)

  ROOT CAUSE:
  ──────────
  Content in blog-posts.json had HTML + markdown mixed:
  <p>The **Mid Journey** AI is amazing</p>
  
  The marked() library doesn't process **text** inside HTML tags!

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ THE SOLUTION                                                             │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  TWO-LAYER FIX:
  
  Layer 1: PREPROCESSING (in formatContent)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────
  BEFORE marked() processes content:
  
  Input:  <p>The **Mid Journey** AI is amazing</p>
           ↓ (regex conversion)
  Output: <p>The <strong>Mid Journey</strong> AI is amazing</p>
  
  
  Layer 2: SAFETY NET (in enhanceContentFormatting)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
  AFTER all processing:
  
  1. Check for remaining **text**
  2. Convert to <strong>text</strong>
  3. Add inline styling: style="font-weight: 700; color: inherit;"
  
  
  RESULT:
  ───────
  <strong style="font-weight: 700; color: inherit;">Mid Journey</strong>
  
  Displays in browser: Mid Journey (properly bold!)
